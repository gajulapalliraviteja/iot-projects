In a transistor used as a switch, it operates in two regions:

1️⃣ Cut-off Region

Base current = 0

Base-Emitter junction is not forward biased

Transistor is OFF

Collector current = 0

Acts like an open switch

Load (LED) is OFF

2️⃣ Saturation Region

Base current is sufficient

Both Base-Emitter and Base-Collector junctions are forward biased

Transistor is fully ON

Acts like a closed switch

Maximum collector current flows

Load (LED) glows
